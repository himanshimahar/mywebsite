About Tanakpur

Tanakpur is a beautiful town located in Champawat district of Uttarakhand, India. Known for its scenic beauty and proximity to the Sharda River, it's a gateway to the Kumaon region.

Swachh Nagar Initiative

Swachh Nagar Tanakpur aims to make our town cleaner and greener through community participation. Report issues and help us maintain hygiene standards.

Future Plans for Cleanliness

  • Expand waste management systems with smart bins and recycling centers
  • Implement regular cleanliness drives in public areas
  • Promote eco-friendly practices and awareness programs
  • Partner with local authorities for better sanitation infrastructure
  • Use technology for real-time monitoring and quick response
